• PICK A MODE

    • Modern: Draw faster by clicking and dragging your mouse around.
    • Traditional: Use the knobs to draw, just like on a real Etch-a-Sketch.

  • PICK A COLOR

    Though the original Etch-a-Sketch only uses black, feel free to use any amount of colors you'd like.

  • PICK A SIZE

    Any number between 1 and 50 works.

    Be careful, choosing a new grid size will erase your current work.
